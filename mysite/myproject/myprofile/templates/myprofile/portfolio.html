Portfolio

My portfolio highlights a decade of leadership in educational development, marked by significant achievements and innovations. I've successfully led multi-stakeholder initiatives that resulted in a crucial compact with the Global Partnership for Education and secured over USD 300 million in direct donor support for educational projects. My oversight extended to all Ministry of Education projects, including those worth over USD 200 million supported by development partners. I spearheaded the creation of Liberia's new Education Sector Plan and conducted comprehensive sector analyses. Innovatively, I led the digitization of educational data collection tools and managed the Annual School Censuses from 2019 to 2022. I established national School Quality Standards and a literacy and numeracy assessment system for key grades. During the COVID-19 pandemic, I directed Liberia's educational response, launched the dormant Liberia Institute for Science and Technology, and developed strategies for STEM prioritization and disaster risk management in education.
